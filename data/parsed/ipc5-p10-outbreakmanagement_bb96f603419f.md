### SECTION: Outbreak Management

- **EFFECTIVE DATE:** February 18, 2025
- **REVIEWED DATE:** February 11, 2025
- **APPROVED BY:** Chief Medical Officer
- **MODIFIED DATE:** May 30, 2025

----

## POLICY

Homes will be proactive in the identification and prevention of outbreaks.

## STANDARD

In the event of an outbreak, a home-specific outbreak management plan will be in place and will be implemented as expeditiously as possible to interrupt further transmission of the etiological agent.

- Outbreaks will be reported to the Regional Director for the home, Extendicare Head Office, and health authority, as required.
- All homes will have an annually reviewed home-specific outbreak plan in place to guide infection prevention and control practices and interventions in the event of an active outbreak.
- All homes will follow the provincial/regional outbreak management protocols as applicable.
