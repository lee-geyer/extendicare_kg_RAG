## POLICY

### Cleaning and Organization

## PROCEDURE

- Clean the baseboards on a frequency that will provide sanitation and preserve the surfaces.

### Collect the following supplies:
- bottle with stripper solution
- bottle with all-purpose cleaner
- scraper
- doodlebug or baseboard cleaning equipment
- bucket with clean water
- clean cloths

### WASHING BASEBOARDS:
- Wipe with all-purpose cleaner.
- Scrub with doodlebug or baseboard cleaning tool.
- Scrape hard soils with scraper.
- Rinse with clean water.
- Dry with cloth.
- Remove all excess water left on the floor.

### STRIPPING BASEBOARDS:
- Apply stripper solution. Protect floor with old blanket.
- Wait a few minutes for the solution to soften floor finish. Up to 5-10 minutes for heavy build-up.
- Scrub with doodlebug or baseboard cleaning tool.
- Use scraper to remove heavy build-up.
- Rinse with clean water and wipe dry.
- Remove all excess water left on the floor.
