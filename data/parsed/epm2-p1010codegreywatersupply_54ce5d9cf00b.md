## POLICY
### Emergency Response Codes

## PROCEDURE
Code Grey, water supply is announced when there is an issue with the water that limits, ceases, or contaminates the home’s water supply, and/or makes the water unpotable and has the potential to affect health and safety.

- Follow the Boil Water Advisory Information Sheet to respond to boil water advisories.
- Note: Information provided from the local public health authority or municipality shall supersede the Code Grey – Water Supply: Boil Water Advisory Information Sheet.
- Follow direction from the Incident Manager.
- Incident Manager and designated IMS Operations Manager - follow the procedures for a water supply disruption as per the designated Job Action checklists.
