## POLICY

Extendicare Medical Affairs is a function operating within the Chief Medical Office, which oversees all administrative activities of Physicians and Nurse Practitioners.

## STANDARD

All Extendicare homes are responsible for following the procedures established by Medical Affairs including but not limited to recruitment, credentialing, onboarding, retainment, compliance, and departure of Medical Directors, Attending Physicians, and Nurse Practitioners.
