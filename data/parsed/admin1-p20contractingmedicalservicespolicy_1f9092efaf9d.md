## POLICY
All Extendicare homes must enter into a written agreement with each medical practitioner for the provision of medical services in the home. Homes must also appoint a physician as the Medical Director for the home. A Medical Director accepts accountability for the overall quality, coordination, and evaluation of medical services, consults with the management team and staff concerning any aspect of the provision of medical services, and provides clinical leadership in medical research and education.

## STANDARD
The written agreement for each medical practitioner must include, at minimum:
- The terms of the agreement;
- The responsibilities of the home;
- The responsibilities and duties of the medical practitioner, including accountability to the Medical Director for adhering to the company’s policies, the provision of medical services, and the provision of regular hours, after-hours, and on-call coverage to ensure residents have access to medical services 24 hours a day, 7 days per week; and
- Where applicable, the name of the physician with whom an extended class registered nurse has a consultative relationship.

Medical practitioners include Medical Directors, Attending Physicians, and Nurse Practitioners.
